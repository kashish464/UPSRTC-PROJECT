At a Glance

UPSRTC is a public sector passenger road transport corporation providing services in the state of Uttar Pradesh and other adjoining states in North India. With a fleet size of over 11485 buses we operate over 123.35cr. kilometers catering to the travel needs of over 43.29cr. people and earning over Rs.4473.70 cr per annum . Providing proper, efficient, well co-ordinated, comfortable and economical Road Transport Services to our passengers, along with earning enough for self-sustenance & growth, is our motto.
